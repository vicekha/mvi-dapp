How it works

Four steps to a trustless swap

No liquidity pools. No AMMs. Direct wallet-to-wallet settlement powered by on-chain order matching.

01
Create an order
Specify what you have and what you want. Set amounts, target chain, and expiry. ETH and native tokens are held in escrow during the order lifetime — ERC-20s and NFTs stay in your wallet.
02
Reactive matching
The Euler-Lagrange order processor instantly scans for opposing orders. For cross-chain swaps, the Reactive Network RSC listens for events on every registered chain simultaneously.
03
Atomic settlement
When a match is found, both sides execute atomically. On same-chain swaps this is a single transaction. Cross-chain swaps use the RSC to trigger callbacks on each chain independently.
04
Expiry & refunds
Unmatched orders expire automatically. Escrowed native tokens are refunded to your wallet. Partial fills are supported — get what's available, rebook the rest up to 3 times automatically.
Asset custody

Your assets, your terms.

Different asset types have different custody models. Understanding this is key to using AutoSwap confidently.

Native tokens
ETH & native gas
When you place an order offering native ETH (or MATIC, etc.), the contract holds it in escrow for the duration of your order. You cannot spend it elsewhere until the order resolves.
⚠
Escrowed immediately. Held by WalletSwapMain until matched, cancelled, or expired. Then automatically refunded.
ERC-20 tokens
USDC, WETH & more
ERC-20 tokens never leave your wallet until the moment of settlement. You grant an approval, and the contract only pulls tokens when a match executes — not before.
✓
Stays in your wallet. The contract checks your balance and allowance at match time. No lockup, no capital inefficiency.
NFTs (ERC-721)
NFT swaps
Like ERC-20s, NFTs remain in your wallet with an approval granted to the contract. The transfer executes atomically at match time. Partial fills are not supported for NFTs — it's all-or-nothing.
✓
Stays in your wallet. Full-amount match required. Approval-based transfer at settlement only.
Features

Everything on-chain, nothing trusted.

🔀
Market sweep matching
When you place an order, the contract immediately sweeps the order book for up to 20 matching orders in the same transaction. Instant fills at the best available rate.
⛓
Cross-chain orders
Offer ETH on Base, receive USDC on Arbitrum. The Reactive Network RSC listens for OrderInitiated events across all registered chains and matches them automatically.
📊
Euler-Lagrange fill optimization
The VirtualLiquidityPool uses an adaptive Euler-Lagrange algorithm to calculate optimal fill amounts based on historical liquidity rate-of-change. Smoother fills, less impact.
🔁
Automatic rebooking
Large orders that expire partially filled can rebook up to 3 times automatically with a shortened duration. Mega orders rebook at just 10% fill threshold — protecting your capital.
🖼
NFT ↔ token swaps
Swap ERC-721 NFTs for tokens, tokens for NFTs, or NFTs for NFTs. The matcher handles all four asset-type permutations with exact tokenId matching for NFT orders.
⏱
Partial fills
ERC-20 orders fill incrementally as matching liquidity arrives. Track your filled amount in real time. Orders stay active and searchable until fully matched or expired.
