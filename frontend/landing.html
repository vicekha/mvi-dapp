What Makes AutoSwap Different

Built Different. By Design.

No Liquidity Pool

Traditional swaps route through pools — you pay slippage, you feed MEV bots. AutoSwap matches intents directly between wallets. There is no pool to drain.

Non-Custodial by Design

ERC-20 tokens and NFTs never leave your control until a match is confirmed. They are not deposited. They are not locked. The contract only executes on mutual consent.

Native Token Escrow

Native gas tokens (AVAX, ETH, S, FLR) are held in escrow — but only until your order matches, expires, or you cancel. You set the window. You retain cancellation rights.

Cross-Chain, In-Chain

AutoSwap works within a single chain and across chains (Sonic ↔ Flare, Songbird). Cross-chain swaps use Reactive Network's event-driven contracts — no bridge, no wrapped token.

1-to-1, 1-to-Many, Many-to-1

One wallet can fulfill multiple counterparty orders in a single transaction. Aggregate demand. Batch fulfill. Split orders. The matcher handles complexity.

NFT ↔ Token ↔ NFT

Any asset class to any asset class. You want to swap a PFP for 500 USDC and a game item? Set the intent. The protocol doesn't care about asset type.

Cross-Chain

Cross-chain without bridges.

Your AVAX stays on Sonic. Their FLR stays on Flare. AutoSwap's Reactive Network contracts coordinate the atomic release on both ends simultaneously. Neither party can receive without the other sending. It's mathematically trustless.
