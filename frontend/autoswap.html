Why AutoSwap

No Liquidity Pool

Your swap never touches a pool. Intents match directly between wallets. No slippage. No pool fees. No liquidity to drain.

Non-Custodial ERC-20 & NFTs

ERC-20 tokens and NFTs are never deposited. The contract holds approval only — transfer executes atomically on match.

Native Token Escrow

Native tokens (ETH, AVAX, S, FLR) are held in escrow from submission. Returned instantly on cancel or expiry.

Cross-Chain, No Bridge

Swap Sonic ↔ Flare without bridges or wrapped tokens. Reactive Network coordinates atomic release on both chains.

1-to-Many & Many-to-1

Aggregate multiple counterparty orders or split a single order across many fulfillers. Batch execution in one transaction.

Any Asset Class

Token↔Token, Token↔NFT, NFT↔NFT. Any ERC-20, ERC-721, or ERC-1155 asset. You define the terms.

How a Swap Works

STEP 1: Set Your Intent

Choose what you're sending, what you want in return, and your expiry window. All asset types supported.

STEP 2: Submit On-Chain

Your intent is recorded on-chain. Native tokens enter escrow. ERC-20 and NFT approvals are set — nothing is deposited.

STEP 3: Matching Begins

Reactive Network's event-driven contracts scan for a counterparty intent that satisfies your order.

STEP 4: Atomic Execution

Match found. Both wallets receive simultaneously. If either side fails, neither transfer happens. Mathematically trustless.

STEP 5: No Match?

Cancel anytime before a match. Order expiry returns your escrowed tokens automatically. Zero penalties.

Cross-chain without compromise.

Your AVAX stays on Sonic. Their FLR stays on Flare. AutoSwap's Reactive Network contracts coordinate atomic release on both ends. Neither party receives without the other sending.
